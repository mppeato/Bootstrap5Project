About

A medical and romantic drama centered around Meredith Grey, an aspiring surgeon and daughter of one of the best surgeons, Dr. Ellis Grey. Throughout the series, Meredith goes through professional and personal challenges along with fellow surgeons at Seattle Grace Hospital.

Setting

Although Grey's Anatomy is almost entirely set in Seattle, Washington, very little of the series is actually shot there. In the earlier seasons, there were more filming locations in Seattle, however as the show went on, more and more sets were built on the sound stages in Los Angeles, California.

Bootstrap 5 Grid

The Bootstrap 5 Grid component I've decided to incorporate below allow for equal-width areas within two rows. The top row divides the full width into two equal areas and the row beneath it allows for the full width to be divided into three equal areas. In my case and for other projects this could be a great way to display multiple photos to showcase what your site has to offer in a professional and easy flexible layout. It's also nice as the grid layout I have chosen is usable and fluid for all device viewports from mobile to desktop.


Navigation

The two examples I chose to incorporate as examples is a navigation with tabs as well as a sticky navigation on the bottom. The tab example was added with a simple class '.nav-tabs'. Having a tabbed interface allows users to reach their intended destination with ease as there is a larger area for them to click on in comparison to having to be exact upon clicking on the words in normal navigation systems. The second navigation example I provided was one with a sticky navigation as it will always appear on the bottom of the screen no matter where a user might be on my page. This allows users to click from page to page without having to scroll back up to the top of the page to the main navigation area. This was added through the use of the 'nav bar fixed-bottom bg-body-tertiary' class. As an added bonus, both of the examples I provided are both responsive allowing users with different screen sizes to use this site and the nav will adjust accordingly.
